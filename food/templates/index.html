Our Mission

Zero Wastage Of Food!
To connect the food donators and the people in need.
Raise awareness about food loss and food waste amongst citizens and bring about behavioural change in them to prevent food waste through guidance and good practices
Raise awareness amongst food businesses by encouraging them to adopt best practices to reduce food loss and food waste in their supply chain, production and distribution cycle
Provides a path to support food waste managem and waste reduction initiatives through coordinated efforts.

Sustainable Development Goal 2 – Zero Hunger – pledges to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture, and is the priority of the World Food Programme.And we are very happy to work in alignment with the SDG-2.

FAQ

What is Micham Amirdham?

Micham Amiradham literally translates to 'what is unconsumed by one, might be a primary need of another'.

Micham Amirdham is an edible food Recovery Project which serves the surplus excess foods from Weddings , Parties , and other events, to the hungry and deprived. This is achieved by contacting through phone number with one another.

Here the receivers can check the availability based on area and can get the food from the donators directly.

2

How MA is different from others?

Micham Amirdham aka MA has two unique features: Provides a contact between the donators and the receivers through phone number and chat. It is a platform where the receivers are notified about the food availability based on the different areas.

Micham Amirdham aka MA is a non-profit organisation which connects food donators with the people in need.
